New to Tibia?

New Player
Where to start?
  • - First you'll need to download a Client Loader.
  • - Then add 'thenewrookgaard.com' as IP.
  • - Make sure the port is 7171.
  • - Select Tibia 8.6 from the list of available protocols and lunch!.

Create your account
  • - Once the client is running, log in using '1' as the Account number and Password.
  • - Login selecting the Account Manager.
  • - Follow the steps to create your account and first player.
  • - Logout and log back in using your new Account number and Password.
  • - Select your player and go wild!.


What's different?

  • - Training monks, depots and mail system
